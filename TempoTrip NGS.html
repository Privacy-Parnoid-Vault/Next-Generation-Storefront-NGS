Fare Categories
Basic
  • Basic
    Basic economy class seats. Seat pitch of less than 34 inches. These fares usually have limitations on carry on baggage, ticket changes, seat selection, boarding priority, and upgrades.
Standard
  • Standard
    Standard economy class seats. Seat pitch of less than 34 inches. Typically allows for carry on baggage, seat selection, and ticket changes for a fee.
Enhanced
  • Enhanced
    Enhanced economy class seats. Seat pitch of 34 inches or greater. Typically allows for carry on baggage and seat selection.
Premium
  • Premium
    First, business, or premium class fares. Seat is a recliner with at least a 36-inch pitch.
Luxury
  • Luxury
    First or business class fares. Seats will include a lie flat seat or pod.
Luxury+
  • Luxury Plus
    Airline seats that have a lie flat suite with aisle access.
